## 1. Overview

Trinity N1B0 is a Neural Processing Unit (NPU) SoC designed for AI/ML inference and training workloads. The chip is organized as a 4×5 2D mesh grid of heterogeneous tiles interconnected by a Network-on-Chip (NoC) fabric.

Key characteristics:
- 20 nodes total (4 columns × 5 rows)
- 12 Tensix compute tiles (organized in clusters of 4)
- 4 NoC-to-AXI bridges (NIU)
- 2 Dispatch engines (East/West)
- 14 DM complexes (8 cores per cluster)
- EDC ring topology for error detection/correction
- Multi-clock domain architecture: AI, NoC, DM, AXI
- Dynamic routing enabled (EnableDynamicRouting = 1'b1)

Note: `trinity_router` is NOT instantiated in N1B0 variant (EMPTY by design).
